About Us

To supply our customers quality drill pipe and accessories—at a competitive rental rate—with the service and support that exceed what you might expect.


Drill Pipe Repair is a reliable provider for the drilling industry, offering top-notch pipe rental and expert repair services. Our facilities, positioned strategically in Texas and Colorado, are fully equipped with a wide range of drill pipe and essential support equipment. Our Texas and Colorado locations are fully functional pipe yards, dedicated to delivering high-quality products promptly.


Drill Pipe Repair is backed by a team of experienced oilfield veterans and experts in engineering, operations, and sales, ensuring that our clients always receive exceptional support. Specializing solely in repair services, we streamline our clients' operations by offering a focused, expert solution, eliminating the complexities of dealing with multiple suppliers. Our commitment is to straightforward and fair pricing, guaranteeing high-quality repair services that deliver real value.

DPR Leaders

Our company leaders boast extensive experience in the oil and gas service industry. With over 100 years of combined experience working closely with oilfield companies, the team at Drill Pipe Repair deeply understands the industry's nuances. This wealth of knowledge ensures that we are perfectly positioned to guarantee that our customers receive precisely what they need for uninterrupted production.

Lloyd “Murray” Dallas

Co-Founder and Owner

Murray Dallas has earned extensive field experience and technical training since beginning his career in the oil industry in 1971. He has held supervisory and management positions throughout most of the oil and gas fields in North America, while designing patented oilfield tools that have been used extensively in horizontal well completions. His contributions to the industry have helped countless customers simplify operations while increasing well production.

Robert Starling

Vice President and CFO

Since joining the company in 2014, Robert Starling has taken charge of accounting, financial reporting, treasury, insurance and risk management, taxation, information technology, and human resources. Boasting over 35 years of experience in accounting and related fields, Robert has a comprehensive background working with clients in the oil and gas, petrochemical, and healthcare industries. He holds a BAA in Accounting from the University of Texas and is a licensed CPA in the state of Texas.

Nels Wickander

Nels Wickander

Nels Wickander offers specialized technical engineering services at Drill Pipe Repair. In addition to his role with us, he serves as the president of Wickander & Associates, a consultancy focusing on the mechanical and metallurgical aspects of oilfield, mining, construction, HDD, and water well drill pipe/tool joint design, manufacturing, and application. Nels is a U.S. Army veteran and has studied metallurgical engineering at the University of Minnesota.
