MAZAK SLT 550 CNC LATHE -CHARLIE-

Our SLT 550 Mazak lathes are designed to handle larger components with high production efficiency. At Drill Pipe Repair, our series of Mazak SLT 550s are outfitted with the latest technology from Mazak, capable of chucking parts up to a 32" diameter and accommodating pipe/tubing up to a 10.8" O.D. We've enhanced our capabilities with an 80" long bed, allowing us to work with longer parts during the manufacturing process, which not only improves our service quality but also reduces production times.

MAZAK QUICK-TURN 450 CNC LATHE -ALPHA-

Mazak Quick-Turn 450 lathes are equipped with 40” and 80” beds for manufacturing a variety of sizes, delivering high-quality parts at top production speed. Redneck Pipe Rental has the capabilities to manufacture long parts that require turning with intricate detail on the outside diameter, or complex smaller parts with inside diameter work. The Mazak Quick-Turn 450 holds tight tolerance and runs parts at rates competitors can’t match, and is able to swallow up to 7.5” diameter and chuck on a 22” diameter part.
