PTECH+ Connection™

The PTech+ Connection™, exclusive to Drill Pipe Repair, is a superior torque connection known for its exceptional torsional and fatigue properties. It's specifically designed to outlast and outperform standard connections. The design of the PTech+ Connection incorporates increased critical cross-sectional areas and extended shoulder contact areas, which significantly improve its mechanical properties compared to typical connections. Moreover, the PTech+ Connection benefits from utilizing a 135ksi specified material yield strength (SMYS), further elevating its performance.


This proprietary technology, integral to Drill Pipe Repair's offerings, is engineered and manufactured with the specific goal of maximizing performance. It delivers the strength and power that drilling operators expect, yet does so without the burden of premium pricing. This approach ensures our clients receive top-tier performance and reliability at cost-effective rates.

  • Patented Thread Design
  • Optimal Torque Capability
  • Improved Fatigue Resistance
  • Reduced Peak Stress


PTECH Completion Drill Pipe™

Conventional, industry-standard tubing often poses challenges in extended reach and other critical completion operations. To address these demanding drilling requirements, PTech Completion Drill Pipe, a key offering of Drill Pipe Repair, is designed to provide higher torsional strength, gas-tight seals, and a streamlined connection profile. This specialized drill pipe is engineered to meet the unique challenges of modern drilling environments, ensuring enhanced performance and reliability.


The field-proven PTech Completion Drill Pipe™, a key component of Drill Pipe Repair's portfolio, features a unique combination of metal-to-metal seal connections, including a robust rotary shoulder connection, and the pressure integrity characteristic of a premium connection. This tubing's streamlined design incorporates an oversized inside diameter across both the connection and tube. This strategic configuration is specifically engineered to maximize hydraulic efficiency while minimizing any risk of obstruction, ensuring smooth and effective operations.


PTECH Completion Drill Pipe is available in 2-3/8” and 2-7/8” diameter sizes.

PTECH High-Torque Drill Pipe™


The PTech High-Torque Drill Pipe™, an essential part of Drill Pipe Repair's inventory, is widely recognized as the go-to industry solution for challenging drilling scenarios like long laterals. It features a patented design that includes a robust rotary shoulder connection. This unique connection offers a significantly higher torsional capacity compared to standard connections of similar size. It also meets the most rigorous safety and dependability standards, making it ideal for drilling operations in tough, high-risk environments. The increased torsional capacity of the PTech High-Torque Drill Pipe substantially enhances the life of the tool joint by withstanding more OD wear. This results in superior, enduring performance that outmatches both API and other premium drill pipes.


PTECH High-Torque Drill Pipe is available in 4”, 4.5”, 5” and 5.5” diameter sizes.
