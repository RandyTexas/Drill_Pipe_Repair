Pipe Repair

Drill Pipe Repair is a leading provider in the manufacturing and repair of oil and gas tubulars. We offer competitive market pricing and straightforward monthly invoicing to simplify operations management for our clients. By conducting repairs in our proprietary machine shops, we ensure complete control over the entire process, ensuring that our tool joints are more durable—thereby extending the lifespan of the drill pipe. Our expertise lies in API drill pipe repair, high-performance drill pipe, drill collars, sub repair, and casing repair, including premium casing connections. We are known for our quick turnaround times, typically completing repairs within just 2-3 days.


From the moment the drill pipe is on the rig, our customers can have peace of mind knowing they are working with high-quality materials, free from the risks of poor management or oversight. With Drill Pipe Repair, you always receive unmatched service and support throughout the lifespan of our products. While we no longer offer rental services, our commitment to quality remains steadfast, as evidenced by our previous practice of being the only company to use 135,000 psi tubulars for pipe accessories, a testament to our dedication to superior standards in the industry.
